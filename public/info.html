Our Features

Turnitin+ offers advanced plagiarism detection and content originality checking to help you ensure your work is authentic and properly cited.

Advanced Document Analysis

Our system analyzes your document against billions of web pages, academic papers, and publications to ensure originality.

Real-Time Results

Get instant feedback on your document's originality score and detailed analysis of potential matching sources.

Accurate Detection

Our advanced algorithms can detect even the most subtle forms of plagiarism, including paraphrasing and text manipulation.

How It Works

Our platform uses sophisticated algorithms to analyze your document and compare it against a vast database of sources. The system identifies matching text and provides you with a comprehensive report highlighting potential issues.

The originality score represents the percentage of your document that appears to be unique. A higher score indicates more original content, while a lower score suggests potential plagiarism concerns.
